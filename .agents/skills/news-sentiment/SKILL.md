# News sentiment

Use **Fresh headlines** and any news sentiment scores in the prompt.

## Rules

- Prefer citing specific headlines when explaining recent moves
- Do not invent headlines, sources, or publication times
- Distinguish company news from macro/sector noise
- Note when the news window returned no relevant articles

## Output style

- Quote or paraphrase headlines with enough detail to verify
- Separate confirmed facts from speculative interpretation
